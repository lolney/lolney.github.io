Projects

Some of the projects I’ve worked on in my spare time, in roughly descending order of seriousness.

Rust Bittorrent Client

An implementation of the bittorent spec in Rust – including a parser for bencoded objects – and a client with a UI built with ncurses. Writeup on some of the implementation challenges coming.

Plantary Surfaces

Using a crude WebGL engine, shows what the solar cycle looks like at various points of the year on a planet with adjustable orbital parameters. E.g., on a planet with an orbital inclination of 90 degrees - tilted on its side - everywhere on the planet but the equator experiences polar night for some part of the year.

World Climate Data

Maps monthly average high/low temperatures, based on interpolated and altitude-adjusted USGS station data.

Zosterops Games Package

Some silly games made in Unity, themed around a type of birds called white-eyes. The first one: navigate to the top of Mt. Kilamanjaro while freeing the white-eyes trapped in cages before a wandering cat gets to them. The second: a kind of tower defence game where you place obstacles to hinder the enemy (cats) while boosting your friends (white-eyes).

In early stages:

Scripting RPG

An attempt to make a sort of MMORPG where your character is controlled entirely with scripts.

Twitter Profiles

Topic analysis of Twitter accounts. Catagorizes interests based on a hierarchy derived from the Wikipedia category graph.
