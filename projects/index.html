Projects

Some of the projects I’ve worked on in my spare time, in roughly descending order of seriousness.

Rust BitTorrent Client

An implementation of the BitTorrent spec in Rust – including a parser for bencoded objects – and a web client with a UI built with React/Redux. Writeup on some of the implementation challenges coming.

Planetary Surfaces

Using a crude WebGL engine, shows what the solar cycle looks like at various points of the year on a planet with adjustable orbital parameters. E.g., on a planet with an orbital inclination of 90 degrees - tilted on its side - everywhere on the planet but the equator experiences polar night for some part of the year.

World Climate Data

Maps monthly average high/low temperatures, based on interpolated and altitude-adjusted USGS station data.

Zosterops Games Package

Some silly games made in Unity, themed around a type of birds called white-eyes. The first one: navigate to the top of Mt. Kilamanjaro while freeing the white-eyes trapped in cages before a wandering cat gets to them. The second: a kind of tower defense game where you place obstacles to hinder the enemy (cats) while boosting your friends (white-eyes).

In early stages:

Bottleneck

A multiplayer game, built with websockets, Node.js, and React, in which players unlock resources by solving programming problems.

Twitter Profiles

Topic analysis of Twitter accounts. Categorizes interests based on a hierarchy derived from the Wikipedia category graph.
